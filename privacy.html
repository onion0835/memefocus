Privacy Policy

MemeFocus is currently a static website. It does not provide user accounts, comments, payments, or sign-in features.

If you contact MemeFocus by email, the information you send is used only to read and respond to your message. The site may later add privacy-friendly analytics to understand page performance and search traffic.

Future meme embeds may load content from third-party platforms. Those platforms can apply their own privacy policies when embedded content is viewed or interacted with.
